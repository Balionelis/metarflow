Privacy

This website uses localStorage to store your recent airport searches locally in your browser. This data is stored only on your device and is never sent to any server. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or by using your browser's privacy settings. No personal information is collected or stored.

This website does not use any tracking, analytics, or cookies. No data is collected about your usage of this website.

Data Sources

METAR data is fetched from aviationweather.gov and is displayed for educational purposes only. This data should not be used for flight planning or any operational aviation purposes.
